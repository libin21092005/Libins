LIBIN MATHEW L

Hi, I’m Libin Mathew L, a Computer Science graduate from Loyola College of Arts and Science. While my academic journey has been rooted in technology, my true passion lies behind the camera — in the world of filmmaking.

Over the years, I’ve directed and produced several short films, many of which have been recognized and awarded for their creativity and impact. Storytelling through visuals is where I find my voice, and I strive to bring emotions, ideas, and perspectives to life through my lens.

  • Served as the Media Secretary for my department
  • Contributed as a Media Team Member for college-wide events
  • Captured and curated memories from various academic and cultural programs

With a blend of technical knowledge and creative vision, I aim to explore the intersection of tech and art — always ready to take on new projects and collaborations that challenge and inspire me.
